Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Ghostsky is a personal, single-user client for the AT Protocol (the network Bluesky runs on). It is not a company, product, or service offered to the public — it exists for one person's private use.

What this site collects

Nothing. This deployment does not run analytics, error tracking, telemetry, or any third-party tracking scripts of any kind. No usage data, device information, or behavioral data is collected, stored, or sent anywhere by this site itself.

What actually happens when you use it

Ghostsky is a client for the AT Protocol network. When you log in and use it, your posts, likes, follows, and other actions are sent directly to your Personal Data Server (PDS) and the wider AT Protocol network — the same way they would be through any AT Protocol client, including the official Bluesky app. That data is governed by the actual AT Protocol network and your PDS provider's own policies, not by this site.

Local storage

Your session and preferences are stored locally in your browser (localStorage), the same as any web app. Nothing here is transmitted to a server operated by this site.

Who operates this

This is a private, self-hosted fork of the open-source Bluesky client, operated by one person for their own personal use. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Bluesky PBC.
