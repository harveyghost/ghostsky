Help

Ghostsky is a personal client for the AT Protocol network — there's no support desk or ticketing system behind it, since it's built and run by one person for their own use.

Protocol-level questions

For questions about how the underlying network works (not this specific client), the official AT Protocol documentation is the best resource: atproto.com.

Account issues

Since Ghostsky connects to your existing AT Protocol account, account-level issues (login, password resets, your PDS) are handled by your account provider, the same as they would be for any client.
